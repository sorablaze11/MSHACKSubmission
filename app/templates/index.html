Dr. Phil

The Product

Dr. Phil is a web application that determines current locations and services based on community health infrastructure facets of health, fitness, and nutrition. Individual (patient specific) address can be input into the application and surrounding health infrastructures—health clinics, supermarkets, fitness centers, and other services—can be visualized.
